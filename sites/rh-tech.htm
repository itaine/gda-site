Job Description

Robert Half Technology's client, a Fort Worth based Marketing and Advertising Firm, has engaged us to source and attract on their behalf three (3) Sr. Web Developer / Design candidates to assume roles in support of a newly signed client. The successful candidates will be part of a team using highly complex concepts to design flows, features, and a user interface that simplifies the experience and attracts consumers.

Roles and Responsibilities:

- Using a
user centered design philosophy and associate techniques, conceptualize, prototype, design and develop aesthetically pleasing and technically fungible web advertising collateral and sites.

- Focus on usability research and testing and heuristic studies to prove your design hypothesis.

- Implement a strong web platform driving the successful ascension of business for our client's client.

Required Skill and Experience:

- Bachelors in Interactive / Web Design or CS/MIS

- 5+ years of front end development experience in
HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery. Candidate
must NOT have skills focused on WYSIWYG mastery and must be able to demonstrate an exceptional ability to "hand code"

- 5+ years of C#, ASP.NET and Sharepoint for Web Applications Development experience allowing them to be a significant factor in the business rules development of the web collateral.

- Prior experience with high traffic websites and content delivery networks


Desired Skills and Experience:

-
Experience with user centered design using Adobe CS4 with a well defined personal design aesthetic that does not overbear the requirements of a style guide.

-
Experience in the creation of comps in vector and .png formats using Illustrator, Photoshop, Fireworks, etc. - Candidate will be asked to provide a Web Portfolio.

- Mastery of a user centered design philosophy and associated techniques including development of user personas and use cases

- Significant experience with
Mobile UI design and front end development.

- Demonstrable understanding of usability research / testing and performing heuristic / human factors studies

-
Familiarity with GIT

-
Experience in an Agile or SCRUM
