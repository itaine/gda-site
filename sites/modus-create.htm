Passion is what drives the best developers. After all, thats why you started playing with computers years ago. Thats exactly how we started Modus Create and how we got to be so successful.  Join our team and satisfy your passion.

Though Modus Create is located in the Washington, DC area, it doesnt really matter where you are, as long as you can get online and want to boost your talent to its best.

If youre like us, you always wanted to say I cant believe they pay me for this. Heres your chance.

As our UI Developer you appreciate good design and have the skills to implement it.  You can take a style guide and make design oriented decisions in your development.  Our UI Developer will be working beside our development team creating customer facing rich interfaces for our high-profile clients, worldwide.


For this role, You Must Have:

  • 3+ years hands experience with

    designing and implementing application design with HTML5/CSS3.

  • 2+ years

    developing application functionality with JavaScript

  • Hands on experience utilizing

    new CSS3 properties

  • Experience with writing targeted

    media queries for multiple devices

  • Experience with

    SASS and Compass. Any other
    pre-processor     experience is a plus.

  • Experience using

    Git as a version control system

  • Familiarity with a responsive framework such as Foundation (brownie

    points if youve rolled your own)

It would be nice if you...

  • expertise in Adobe CS Design applications:

    Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Fireworks

  • have used Grunt

  • familiarity with the

    command line

  • front-end package management such as bower.

  • be experienced

    collaborating on small teams in an agile software environment

  • be passionate about

    good design and web standards

  • have knowledge and exposure to Senchas frameworks

  • have a

    Dribbble, Behance or
    other portfolio site     showcasing your design skills

  • have consulting experience


Are you not actually looking for a job? How would you like:

  • Competitive compensation & benefits

  • Company-provided, top notch hardware, software and services

  • Ability to expense technical books

  • GitHub repos for your projects

  • A chance to get your personal projects sponsored

  • Travel if you like, stay at home if you dont

  • Connect with your colleagues

  • Assisted continuous self-development

  • Work with the best Sencha team outside of Sencha

  • Talk at meetups

  • Attend internal hackathons and win awards
